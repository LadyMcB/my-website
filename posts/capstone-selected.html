I Finally Settled on a Business Idea for my MBA Capstone Project!

November 3rd, 2023 (Friday)

For my final project at Quantic, I get to write a business plan. When I found out this was the final, I was SUPER disappointed. What a boring way to end a boring course of study! (Can you tell how thrilled I've been about the whole thing so far?)

Anyway, for weeks I've been stewing about what business I want to write up. It's got to be something I'm actually interested in, or I know I'll never be motivated enough to actually get the work done.

I was designing an alter ego as part of a mindset module within a course I'm taking on social media content creation, and I suddenly remembered a little poem I wrote called "I Am Flowing Woman" for a women's book club I was participating in before the baby was born. I decided it was the perfect inspiration for the alter ego exercise, and as I was taking in the energy of the woman described in the poem an idea for a whole lifestyle e-commerce store around Flowing Woman emerged.

So, coming soon: Flowing Woman, a store for the cosmic feminine. Can't wait to share more.
